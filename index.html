How worthy are your cats ?

Dr. Evil asks you for money. You don't have any money but cats. Either you give him your cats or you die.

There are four famous cats below. Each cats worth some money. By clicking on each cat you will add a specific amount of money to current money you have now.

To not die, you will need to give him your cats and adds up to the price he wants. Once exceed the price, you die immediately because Dr. Evil wants the right amount of cats only.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each cats.
